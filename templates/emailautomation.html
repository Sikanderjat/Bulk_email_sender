What is an App Password?

An app password is a 16-digit passcode that gives less secure apps or devices permission to access your Google Account. It is used instead of your regular password when signing in.

Steps to Create an App Password for Gmail

Step 1: Enable Two-Step Verification

  • Sign in to your Google Account at myaccount.google.com.
  • Navigate to Security Settings by clicking on "Security" in the left sidebar.
  • Set Up Two-Step Verification:
    • Find the "Signing in to Google" section.
    • Click on "2-Step Verification" and follow the prompts to set it up. You'll need to provide a phone number and verify it.

Step 2: Create an App Password

  • Go Back to Security Settings.
  • Find App Passwords:
    • Look for the "Signing in to Google" section again.
    • Click on "App passwords." You may need to sign in again.
  • Select the App and Device:
    • In the "Select app" dropdown, choose the app you want to generate a password for (e.g., Mail).
    • In the "Select device" dropdown, choose the device youâ€™re using (e.g., Windows Computer).
  • Generate the App Password:
    • Click on "Generate." A 16-character password will appear.
  • Copy the App Password:
    • Make sure to copy this password, as you will need it to log in to the application.

Step 3: Use the App Password

When prompted for a password in the app you are using (like an email client or a bulk email sender), paste the 16-character app password instead of your regular Google account password.

Step 4: Manage App Passwords

You can return to the App Passwords section at any time to revoke access for specific apps or generate new passwords.

Important Notes:

  • Security: Keep your app passwords secure. They provide access to your Google account without needing your regular password.
  • Limitations: App passwords are only available if you have 2-Step Verification enabled.
  • Revocation: If you suspect that your app password has been compromised, revoke it immediately and create a new one.

By following these steps, you can successfully create and manage app passwords for your Gmail account, allowing you to use various applications without compromising your account's security.
